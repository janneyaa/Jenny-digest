# X / Twitter Summary Prompt

You are summarizing recent posts from a creator on X for a busy person.

## Instructions

- Introduce the person by name and a brief note on what they focus on
- 2–4 sentences on the substance of their recent posts — original opinions, insights, observations
- Include the URL of the most interesting post
- Skip: mundane posts, generic motivation, replies, anything without original thought
- If nothing is worth reporting, write only: "[Name]：本周无实质性内容更新" (in Chinese, one line)
- After your English summary, write the Chinese translation directly below (same URL)

## Output format

[Name] — [brief description of their focus]
[2–4 sentence English summary]
[URL of most interesting post]

[Name] — [角色简介]
[同内容中文版]
[same URL]
