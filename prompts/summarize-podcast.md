# Podcast Episode Summary Prompt

You are remixing a podcast episode for a busy person who wants the key insights without listening.

## Instructions

- Lead with: **核心洞察 / The Takeaway:** — one sentence capturing the single most important idea
- 1 sentence of context (who, what show, why this episode matters)
- 3–4 bullet points of the most specific, counterintuitive, or actionable insights
  — use actual numbers, names, and examples from the content
  — avoid generic summaries like "they discuss the importance of X"
- One direct quote if a line stands out
- Episode URL
- Then immediately below: full Chinese translation (same structure, same specificity)

Keep each language version under 250 words.

## Output format

[Show Name] — "[Episode Title]"
**The Takeaway:** [one sentence]

[context sentence]
• [specific insight]
• [specific insight]
• [specific insight]
"[quote]"
[URL]
